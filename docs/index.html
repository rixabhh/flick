Workflow

Designed for the moment before you press send.

Flick works from your system tray and replaces text directly in the app you are already using. Emails, notes, docs, chats, issue comments, or code-adjacent writing all stay in flow.

01

Type your draft anywhere.

02

Add a trigger like !fix, !casual, or !translate:hindi.

03

Flick transforms the text in-place and restores your clipboard.

Commands

Small triggers. Big cleanup.

Built-in commands cover everyday writing, while custom commands let you define your own prompt-backed workflows.

!fixGrammar, spelling, punctuation, and awkward phrasing.
!formalProfessional, polished writing for work.
!casualNatural, friendly phrasing for everyday messages.
!shorterCondense long text without losing the point.
!longerExpand short ideas with useful context.
!improveImprove clarity, flow, grammar, and readability.
!rephraseSame meaning, cleaner wording.
!bulletTurn messy notes into scannable bullets.
!explainSimplify complex text into plain language.
!translate:<lang>Translate into another language on demand.
